Company History

MSW Kitchen has provided your communities with some of the greatest-tasting, premium, reasonably priced ice creams. That still holds true for us now as it did back then.

On top of that, we wanted people to taste the legendary dessert that Malaysia people always eat back in the days. This ice cream was famously called Aiskrim Malaysia! But in MSW Kitchen, this ice cream is called 'Aiskrim Gemok'. There are over 24 types of flavors that has been sold these past years.

For this reason, we're dedicated to giving customers an unrivalled experience with our goods, services, availability, and value. Every action we take stems from a single conviction: everybody can provide themselves with the greatest care possible whenever and however frequently they want.

VISION

Establishing MSW Kitchen as a known ice cream supplier throughout Malaysia in the next five years.

MISSION

To produce Malaysian ice cream products that are unique, high quality, and rich in flavouring options to provide consumer satisfaction with every purchase.

MOTTO

'Unleash Your Inner Flavor Explorer'
